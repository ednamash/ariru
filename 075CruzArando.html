Don Cruz preparing the field for the Women's Group garden in Imi.

Cruz makes it look easy, eh? Actually, it is pretty easy in a technical sense, once you get the hang of it. On the other hand, it's more tiring than one might expect. The main advantage of horses over oxen for traction purposes is that they can move pretty fast, as long as you keep reminding them to do so; of course this means you have move pretty fast too.
